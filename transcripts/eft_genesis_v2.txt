Map made for Extreme Football Throwdown.

The gamemode can be found here.

Official Server: connect eft.noxiousnet.com:27015

Map Notes:
- Space map based off Sky Step.
- 10 second Speedball at ballspawn that can be accessed from both the top level and main level.
- 10 second Magnetball that can be activated by running through the green screens.
- Double ring goals, you only need to get through the colored ring for it to count though.
- Plenty of shooting locations.
- Most of the textures were taken from de_star, credit goes to Verctexius for recoloring them.
- Thanks to dissident for helping improve the map.
