Map made for Extreme Football Throwdown.

The gamemode can be found here.

Official Server: connect eft.noxiousnet.com:27015

Map Notes:
- Water Ball lasts 25 seconds.
- Mix of Sky Step and Space Jump, except has run in goals.
- Large pathways for long distance throws.
- Jump pads to take you side to side.
- Teleporters, use the teleporter corresponding to your team color, using an opponent's teleporter will kill you, they also reset the ball.
- Thanks to dissident for tips and doing the 3-D skybox.
