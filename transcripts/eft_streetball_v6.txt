For use of the "Extreme Football Throwdown" gamemode by JetBoom (DOWNLOAD): https://github.com/JetBoom/extremefootballthrowdown

City themed map for EFT

Features:
-Touchdowns are the spinning rings inside the teams' color coated buildings. Walk inside and look up and throw the ball in, or place a well aimed shot from the open window.
-Many cars and buildings to use to your advantage
-Two tunnels that connect the red and blue buildings. Includes a 9 second speed-ball powerup in the middle of the tunnels.
-15 second blitzball powerup located at the black building
