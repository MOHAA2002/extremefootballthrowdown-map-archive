Map Notes:
- Map sports a futuristic hologram theme.
- Speed Ball rings next to the ball spawn, each lasting 7.5 seconds.
- Features 2 ways to score, a throw in and a run in.
- Has jump pads to take you around the map, they also could be used to bunny hop.
